PicPlanr Version 34: Deep Website Analysis

Replace at the main level of GitHub:
- index.html
- app-v32.js
- styles-v32.css

Add this new file in this exact folder:
- api/website/analyse.js

Do not delete or replace any social connection, publishing, Supabase or domain files.

What this version does:
- reads up to six useful public website pages
- analyses customer markets, brand tone and positioning
- shows evidence-led strengths and weaknesses
- reviews trust, calls to action and the customer journey
- gives limited on-page search visibility advice
- creates five content pillars, three quick wins and three first content ideas
- passes the resulting brand profile into later PicPlanr image and caption analysis
- keeps the Account Strength result inside the Account Review page

Testing:
1. Upload the four files, preserving the api/website folder.
2. Wait for Vercel to show Ready.
3. Open the newest unique .vercel.app deployment first.
4. Enter boxhub.uk and click Decode my brand.
5. The analysis should identify the business from website evidence and list the pages explored.

Required Vercel setting:
- OPENAI_API_KEY must already be present.

Optional:
- OPENAI_TEXT_MODEL, otherwise gpt-4.1-mini is used.
